March Events

Sat, March 2 | From 2-4 pm

Meditation in Motion @ Community Lounge, School of Public Health

Meditation in Motion is a health and wellness workshop for university students that combines mindfulness techniques with artistry to improve one's self-being. This workshop is for 2 hours in duration and consists of a 45 minute targeted meditation, 60 minute art project (origami/painting) and a 15 minute QA session with instructors from the Canton Public Library. Students from all specializations are encouraged to attend and enjoy.
